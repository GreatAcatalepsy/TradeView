What exactly is TradeView?

a new way of moving your workspace to the virtual sphere

TradeView started out as a way to visualize 4D simulations of stock volatility. Since, it has grown into an entire virtual interface for the Oculus Rift.  Our team started at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. We are a part of the first VR class in the nation. With guidance from Steve LaValle and Anna Yershova, two former engineers at Oculus. Keep in mind that we're still working and more photos and features will follow shortly! 

Within TradeView, you can:
-Look at 4D visualizations of stock
-Browse the internet
-Have upwards of 12 screens active (fully customizable)
-Utilize headtracking, controllers, and your normal mouse/keyboard
-Integrate with leap motion
-Change backgrounds of your workspace
-Customize the orientation and content of all on screen tools
